Privacy Policy

Wallcraft – Wallpaper & Caption Editor

📅 Effective Date: May 9, 2026 🔄 Last Updated: May 9, 2026 🌐 English / Indonesian

Welcome to Wallcraft ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Wallcraft – Wallpaper & Caption Editor (the "App"). Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the App.

📌 We are committed to protecting your privacy. Wallcraft does not sell your personal data to third parties.

1 Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

a) Information You Provide

  • Caption project names and text content you create within the App
  • Preferences you set (theme, language) — stored locally on your device

b) Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information (device model, OS version, unique device identifiers)
  • App usage data (features used, session duration) — via third-party analytics
  • Advertising identifiers (e.g., Google Advertising ID) — used for personalized ads
  • IP address and general location (country-level) for ad targeting

c) Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or phone number
  • We do not collect passwords or financial information
  • We do not access your contacts or messages

2 How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve the App's features and functionality
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Fix bugs and ensure technical performance
  • Comply with legal obligations

3 Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services, each with their own Privacy Policy:

  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements.
    https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Pexels API — for providing wallpaper images. Wallcraft fetches images from Pexels servers. No personal data is shared with Pexels.
    https://www.pexels.com/privacy-policy/
  • Google Play Services — for app distribution and device-level services.
    https://policies.google.com/privacy
These third-party providers may collect data as described in their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

4 Advertising & AdMob

Wallcraft uses Google AdMob to serve ads. AdMob may use your advertising identifier and other data to show you personalized ads based on your interests.

  • Banner ads — displayed at the bottom of the Wallpaper screen
  • Interstitial ads — shown when opening wallpaper details or before export
  • Rewarded ads — shown when saving in HD quality (user-initiated)
  • Rewarded Interstitial ads — shown as optional bonus during export

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting adssettings.google.com or through your device's advertising settings.

5 Data Storage & Security

  • Your favorites and saved caption projects are stored locally on your device using Hive (local database)
  • We do not operate any external servers to store your personal data
  • Wallpaper images are cached temporarily on your device for performance
  • We implement reasonable technical measures to protect data in transit and at rest
⚠️ No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information.

6 Permissions We Request

The App may request the following device permissions:

  • Storage / Media Access — to save downloaded wallpapers and exported images to your gallery
  • Camera — to capture photos as background in the caption editor (optional)
  • Internet — to fetch wallpapers from Pexels and load advertisements
  • Set Wallpaper — to apply wallpapers directly to your device home/lock screen

All permissions are used solely for the stated purposes. You can revoke permissions at any time in your device settings.

7 Children's Privacy

Wallcraft is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us immediately so we can take appropriate action.

8 Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

Since most data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by clearing the App's cache/data in your device settings, or by using the "Clear Cache" and "Clear Favorites" options within the App's Settings screen.

9 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
